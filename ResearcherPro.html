CITE YOU'RE SOURCES! When writing and stating something that has been said or taken from another website, don’t forget to state the name of the source or the authors last name. Then make sure to elaborate further on that statement you used from a different source and write about how it connects to what you were writing assignment is about. Kind of like the SEE structure evidence and explain are very differen, and you dont want your explaination to be from somewhere else. Quotation is similar to citing, it makes it clear what you were stating is not your word but rather from another source, but don't forget to state from where those exact words you have quoted are coming.

Paraphrasing is something we do a lot when researching, but it's important to understand that it just doesn’t mean switching up the words, but writing the information into your own words also it requires for ou to understand and explain it in a new way without changing the meaning.

Don't be afraid of using plagiarism checkers, Grammarly provides a free plagiarism checker which detects what you have originally written and what has been copy pasted.
